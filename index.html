The Reason

Why we're merging

For too long, our projects have been scattered across different brands, domains, and identities. It's time to bring everything under one roof — with one clear voice.

01
Simplify Everything
Nine separate brands was too many. We're consolidating our media projects into one group — Prism Media Group — so our audience knows exactly who we are and what we stand for.
02
Stronger LGBTQ+ Identity
Femboy Media Corporation brought LGBTQ+ representation to the forefront. Rather than keeping it as one brand among many, we're baking that inclusivity into the DNA of the entire group.
03
Bigger & Bolder Together
United Broadcasting's streaming reach, Anarchy Group's creative edge, and Magic Plum's infrastructure are stronger combined than apart. Prism Media Group is the sum of our best parts.
04
One Identity Layer
Plum Identity is becoming MyPMGI — a single sign-on and account hub for everything Prism. One login to access all our media, tech services, and future platforms.
